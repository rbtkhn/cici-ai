# Voice Surface

**Status: stub** — No governed artifacts yet. Populated through approved proposals.

## Purpose

This surface holds canonical truth about how Cici communicates: tone, style, persona notes, and expression conventions.

## What belongs here (when ready)

- Tone preferences (formal vs. casual, depth vs. brevity)
- Persona notes (how Cici presents itself across different AI clients)
- Expression conventions (terminology preferences, framing habits)
- Context-sensitivity rules (different registers for different situations)

## How to populate

1. Observe and gather evidence in `evidence/` (or note patterns in Supabase memories)
2. Synthesize a draft in `prepared-context/synthesis/`
3. Create a proposal in `proposals/queue/` with `target_surface: "voice"`
4. On approval, add the artifact here

## Related surfaces

- `identity/` — who Cici is (voice is how it expresses that)
- `memory-policy/` — what Cici captures (voice influences framing of captures)
